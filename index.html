The Philosophy

Restraint Over Playfulness.

We believe that true luxury lies not in excess, but in the deliberate curation of every essential component. Strata is born from a desire to strip away the superfluous and expose the pure, beating heart of horology.

Architecture

Exposed Precision.

Every gear, every bridge, every jewel serves a purpose. By suspending the movement within a sapphire and titanium housing, the mechanism becomes the dial.

  • 01 Micro-rotor automatic winding
  • 02 Hand-beveled bridges
  • 03 Free-sprung balance wheel
The Process

Forged by Hand.

01. MILLING

The grade 5 titanium case is CNC milled to exact tolerances, then hand-finished.

02. POLISHING

Every component is black-polished by hand using diamond paste and tin plates.

03. ASSEMBLY

It takes 400 hours for a single master watchmaker to assemble and regulate Strata.
